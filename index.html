The nations greatest natural wonder has taken to it’s social media channels this morning to post an incredible transformation picture. Known internationally for being the most popular, albeit longest podcast in Western society, the Joe Rogan Podcast often has a run time that pushes past the three-hour mark. Although many other Bluetooth headphone manufacturers have claimed their headsets last for up to 18 hours in some cases, it’s been proven time and time again that a Joe Rogan Podcast can only be listened to in its entirety with wired headphones or car stereo.
